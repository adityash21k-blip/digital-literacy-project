Social Media Checklist for College Students
The Do's
1. Do Curate a Professional Image: Use platforms like LinkedIn to highlight your 
academic achievements, projects at VIT Bhopal, and technical skills.
2. Do Use Privacy Settings: Regularly review your account security settings to 
control who can see your personal posts and tagged photos.
3. Do Think Before You Post: Ask yourself if your content could negatively impact 
your future career or university reputation before hitting "share".
4. Do Network Meaningfully: Follow industry leaders, join academic groups, and 
engage in constructive discussions relevant to Computer Science.
5. Do Fact-Check Information: Verify the source of news or academic resources 
before sharing them to prevent the spread of digital misinformation.
